About Macmillan Cancer Support

Macmillan Cancer Support is one of the largest British charities, and provides specialist health care, information and financial support to people affected by cancer. As well as helping with the medical needs of people affected by cancer, Macmillan also looks to help with social, emotional and practical aspects of living with the illness.

Founded in 1911, Macmillan Cancer Support has over a century of experience working with those diagnosed with cancer. Their network of Macmillan nurses, doctors and other health professionals provide expert care, and their information services help people understand their diagnosis and treatment options.

The charity also campaigns to improve cancer care services and advocates for policy changes that will benefit those affected by cancer across the UK.
